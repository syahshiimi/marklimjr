Mark Lim (b.1995) is an
interactive designer that works with
kinetic sculpture
and
sound.

He is intrigued over the
human cognition
human behavior to sound
and
objects.

He is inspired by
playgrounds,
toys and
marble run machines.

His current research
is in the act of
play and
behaviour manipulation
through
interaction design.

Pursuing his curiosity in
analog electronics,
He insists on
demystifying
the use of technology
in artworks.

Making a point to be
transparent about the
technical aspects
of the things
he makes.

Building his knowledge in both
mechanical structures
as well as
digital interfaces,
he is working to be a
versatile technical assistant
to artists who are incorporating technology in their works.
